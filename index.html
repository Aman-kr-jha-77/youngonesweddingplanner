Our Story

Who We Are

Turning Moments Into Memories


Born from a Love of Celebrations

Mangalam Wedding Planner was founded with a single belief: every couple deserves a wedding that feels uniquely, deeply theirs. What began as a small team of passionate planners has grown into a full-service celebration company trusted by hundreds of families across Bihar.

Our founders grew up watching the magic of Bihar weddings, the vibrant colours, the rituals rich with meaning, the joy that fills every corner of the venue. We started this company to preserve that magic while adding our own touch of modern elegance.

Our Philosophy

We believe weddings are not just events — they are the beginning of a lifelong story. We listen carefully, plan meticulously, and execute flawlessly. Every decision we make is guided by one question: "Will this create a memory the couple will cherish forever?"

From the first consultation to the last dance, we are by your side — a trusted partner, a creative collaborator, and most importantly, a team that genuinely cares.
